About The Colour

A club for language, culture, and bright conversation.

At Risk

The real danger is disappearance.

A language is not dangerous because people speak it. In language study, the urgent question is which languages are in danger of disappearing. One of the most fragile examples often cited is Njerep, a critically endangered language of Cameroon with only a tiny number of speakers reported in recent years.

Spotlight Njerep

Critically endangered; reports describe only a few elderly speakers.

Highest risk: fewer speakers, little daily use, and limited transmission to children.
